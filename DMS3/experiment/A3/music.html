ERASHIFT

THE EVOLUTION OF SONG

EraShift is an interactive web project exploring how generative AI interprets and replicates the stylistic evolution of music from the 1950s to the 2020s. Featuring decade-specific songs built from the same lyrics and paired with looping visuals, it critically examines AI’s creative strengths, limitations, and cultural implications in the context of music history. Through this lens, the project invites reflection on how technology reshapes our understanding of genre, authorship, and cultural memory.

The project combines human creativity with a wide range of generative AI tools to produce its music, visuals, and interactive website. From sound design to animation to code, the process involved constant negotiation between human intent and machine-generated content. Developed collaboratively by three students—Jonathan, Xander, and Milo—EraShift serves as a multidisciplinary experiment in design, music, and emerging technology.
